Frequality Asked Questions

  • 1. How can your services help improve our sales process?
    Our visual tools go far beyond static renders — they allow buyers to walk through a home before it’s built. Whether it’s Betagen (Virtual Display Home), ImbadotoVue (Life-size Virtual Home on-site), or Immersa Catalogue, each solution is crafted to create an emotional connection, build trust, and accelerate decision-making. These tools are not only powerful for remote buyers and NRIs, but also incredibly effective for visitors at your sales office or project site. When potential clients experience a home virtually — in full scale or interactive format — they immediately understand the layout, flow, and feel of the space. This leads to fewer doubts, faster decisions, and deeper confidence in your project.
  • 2. We already have 3D models. Can you work with those?
    Absolutely. If you have existing 3D models, we can adapt and enhance them for immersive presentation — saving time and cost. If you don’t, we also offer full modeling services from scratch, based on your 2D floor plans or concepts.
  • 3. Can we use these experiences in our sales office or site location?
    Yes. Our solutions are designed to work on-site or remotely — whether it’s a sales gallery, property expo, or even on the client’s own land. ImbadotoVue is especially powerful on-site, allowing buyers to experience their future home right where it will be built.
  • 4. What devices do clients need to view these experiences?
    Most of our solutions are optimized for mobile devices. For services like Betagen and Immersa Catalogue, a modern smartphone or tablet provides a seamless experience. For ImbadotoVue, due to its life-size, real-time rendering, we recommend a mid to high-end Android device to ensure smooth performance and visual clarity.
  • 5. How do we price these services — and is there flexibility?
    Pricing depends on the scope of the project — whether you're providing a model or we’re building from scratch, the number of homes, and level of interactivity.
