HOW WE WORK

Proven Process

01

Initial Consultation

The Initial Consultation is where we get to meet you(ONLINE OR OFFLINE), see your home, and learn more about what you are trying to accomplish. After the initial consultation, we will develop an estimate for your project which will outline the number of design hours we think it will take to complete your project. If you like us and the estimate and want to work with us, simply sign the estimate and pay your retainer or deposit and we will begin your project. Inspiration Images – During the Initial Consultation, you will provide us with inspiration images. These images could be from anywhere etc.

02

Launch D2C Meeting

Once you have signed on with us, your primary designer will work with you to schedule your Launch D2C Meeting. During the Launch D2C Meeting, we will develop your Project Plan. The idea is to create a Mutually deciding on ideas which bring your vision to. What kind of style detail would accentuate the furnishing example life Mid-Century Modern ,Transitional Styles, Pacific-Northwest Styles, Modern-Rustic or Minimalistic structures. The Project Plan gives you an opportunity to identify top priorities within the project and gives us an opportunity to set expectations for the timeline. The project plan helps us keep your project focused and within budget. And this is the essence of the Launch D2C Meeting . Other than this, your budget and goals are also key features in deciding which design to go with. In a nutshell, we aspire to give you a design which is per your taste without burning a hole in your pocket.

Design Development

This is the phase where the rubber meets the road. We will begin designing the portion of your home that we agreed to when defining the phases of your project at your Launch D2C Meeting. Depending on your project, we may develop design sheets. Our top priority is to give you the best design at a reasonable price. We typically communicate with our digital design sheets.
